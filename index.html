Welcome to the Pikmin-Fan Website

While on an interstellar space vacation, Captiain
Olimar was struck by a meteor and crash landed
on an unknown planet filled with poisonous
oxygen. With the ship parts scattered
throughout the planet, his life-support could only
function for only 30 days. While exploring, he
discovers some creatures called Pikmin. Could
these Pikmin decide Olimars' fate in a good way?

After Pikmin 1, Olimar returns to his planet
Hocoate. He learns from his boss, the president of
Hocoate Freight, sent a new recruiter, Louie to
a mission with Golden Pikpik carrots. However,
According to Louie, he met a ravenous space
bunny, and his entire load of Pikpik carrots
were eaten! The president took a loan to cover
the loss with a remaining 10,000 Poko debt.

The planet Koppai is having a food shortage.
After finding a planet with fruit, Alph, Brittany,
and Captain Charlie were sent to the planet,
PNF-404. After reaching orbit, there was a
malfunction to their ship, the SS Drake, and they
were separted as a result. With a mission to save
their planet, find each other, AND return home,
they have a lot of obstacles to defeat.

Captain Olimar crash lands on the Pikmin planet,
presumably for the first time? After encountering
his interstellar radio, he sends an SOS signal to
the Rescue Corps. However, they suffered the
same fate as Captain Olimar and crash landed.
Now YOU are sent to rescue your fellow Rescue
Corp members, Captain Olimar, and also other
castaways who crash landed.
